Meet the team

and the cats.

Sophie Riopel

Born and raised in a family of artists and craftspeople, Sophie has always had a passion for creativity and the arts. Growing up she always loved experimenting with different forms of art. From starting to draw posters to taking years of voice lessons to learn how to preform, she always wanted to share her talents with the world.

However, she didn't always intend to pursue an artistic career. It wasn't until she was diagnosed with a rare medical condition, causing her to slowly losing her vision, that she decided she needed to follow her dreams while she has the chance.

Determined to continue her work as a graphic designer despite her condition, Sophie began exploring new ways to design that would be inclusive and accessible to all, including those with disabilities. She found that her own experiences as someone with vision loss gave her a unique perspective on the importance of accessibility in design, and she became an advocate for creating designs that are usable and accessible to everyone.

Specialty

Branding | Print & Media | UI & UX

Jack Huynh

Jack was born with a love for technology and a natural talent for art. From a young age, she would spend hours drawing. As she grew up, she became fascinated with how people used art as a way to speak to people, and as technology improved and grew, it became apparent the way forward was through digital media. It wasn't long before she discovered her true calling as a graphic designer.

Aside from her work as a graphic designer, Jack is an avid gamer and often examines how people use graphic design to elevate their games. She also has a fond love of cats and incorporates her feline friends into any projects and sketches that lets her.

She is always on the lookout for new inspiration and ways to improve her craft, and she remains dedicated to using her skills to create designs that are both beautiful and functional.

Specialty

Branding | Illustration | Motion
