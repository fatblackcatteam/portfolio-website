Project Goal

The goal of this project was to create a brand identity for Fat Black Cat Studio. This included creating our logo, choosing our brand's colours & typeface, and developing other print & media components.

Project Goal

The goal of this project was to create a brand identity for Fat Black Cat Studio. This included creating our logo, choosing our brand's colours & typeface, and developing other print & media components.

Since this project is the basis of our brand identity, we wanted to ensure all of its components represents our style and allows us to stand-out from the typical graphic design or advertising studio.

Why Fat Black Cat?

When selecting a name for our studio, we wanted to pick something unique. We bounced many ideas around until the saying “I work hard so my cat can have a better life” came to mind. Then we decided to name our studio after our furry team member, Lucky.
