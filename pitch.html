Storyboarding

Our client already had a concept for the video, so our goal with the storyboard was to ensure that we understood their ideas. We first had to figure out the flow and maintain a smooth transition. The sketches show how we're going to move through the video along with the line that our client wanted. This way we're able to make any necessary changes before animating.
