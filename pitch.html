Project Goal

The goal of this project was to create a short promotional video for Pitch, a competitive event hosted by Link North for entrepreneurs.

Pitch is an competitive event where entrepreneurs present their investor pitch to judges for a chance to win prizes, expand their network, and grow their investment opportunities.

Storyboarding

Storyboarding

Our client already had a concept for the video, so our goal with the storyboard was to ensure that we understood their ideas. We first had to figure out the flow and maintain a smooth transition. The sketches show how we're going to move through the video along with the line that our client wanted. This way we're able to make any necessary changes before animating.

Process

Process

Photography Treatments

Our client wanted to use two photographs in the video, but since we were working with stock images, we had to treat them for use in the video. This included removing the backgrounds and adjusting the exposure/sharpness of the photographs.
