Project Goal

Project Goal

The goal of this project was to update Tourism Cochrane's brand identity. This included updating their logo, updating the brand's colours & typeface, and designing website page prototypes.

Tourism Cochrane is the official tourist association of Cochrane, Ontario. Tourism Cochrane focuses on promoting and selling the region as a must-see destination for tourists, sports tournaments, delegates and business travellers.

Tourism Cochrane is the official tourist association of Cochrane, Ontario. Tourism Cochrane focuses on promoting and selling the region as a must-see destination for tourists, sports tournaments, delegates and business travellers.

Brand Creation

Brand Creation

Initial Versions

When updating the logo, the client wanted to keep a similar look and keep elements of the old logo. They explained that the concept of their old logo was to represent nature and rivers.

Logo Evolution

We went through many different versions of the logo during the design process. Since Cochrane Ontario is a region known for it's polar bear habitat and a great place to explore nature, the these elements were both considered for the logo. It was important to them not to overly draw attention
to the polar bear.

Colour Palette

The colour palette of Tourism Cochrane was chosen to reflect the colours present in northen nature. The palette is quite limited and harmonious without any colour standing out to much from the others.

Typeface

Tourism Cochrane has both primary and secondary typefaces. The primary typeface used for headers and large text is Noto Sans JP. The secondary typeface used for paragraphs and small text is Roboto.
